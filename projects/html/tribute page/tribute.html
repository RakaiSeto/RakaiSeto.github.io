James Harden

One of the best Shooting Guard ever

Here's a timeline about James Harden career

  • 2006 - Ranked 1st in California, high school
  • 2008 - Selected for All-Pac-10 1st Team
  • 2009 - Selected for Pac-10 Player of the Year and selected with the third overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft by the Oklahoma City Thunder.
  • 2012 - Received Sixth Man of the Year award and traded to the Houston Rockets
  • 2013 - Making his 1st NBA All-Star appearance, named for All NBA Third Team
  • 2014 - Making his 1st NBA All-Star Starter as replacement for injured Kobe Bryant, named for All NBA First Team
  • 2015 - Making his 1st Conference Finals appearance, named for All NBA First Team, Finished 2nd in MVP voting
  • 2016 - Named for All NBA First Team
  • 2017 - Finished 2nd in MVP voting, named for All NBA First Team
  • 2018 - Wins the MVP award, record the 1st ever 60 point Triple Double against the Orlando Magic
  • 2019 - Finished 2nd in MVP voting, record 32 game streak with atleat 30 points, named for All NBA First Team
  • 2020 - Finished 3rd in MVP voting, named for All NBA First Team
  • 2021 - Traded to the Brooklyn Nets in a massive 4-team deal
